GRE
The Graduate Record Examinations (GRE) General Test is a standardized test that graduate schools use to evaluate candidates.It consists of 5 sections of evaluation. One AWA(Analytical Writing Assessment) section with two topics, Two Quantitaive Resoning(Math) and Two Verbal Reasoning sections. There's also one un-scored experimental section as well. It is evaluated out of 340 for the two reasoning sections and out of 5 for the AWA.
TOEFL
The TOEFL iBT is the Test of English as a Foreign Language Internet-based Test. The TOEFL exam determines if a student — whose native language is not English — has English-language skills that are strong enough to succeed in courses at a college or university in the United States, Canada, United Kingdom, New Zealand, and Australia. The Internet-based exam contains four parts: Reading,Listening,Speaking,Writing. It is evealuted out of 120
IELTS
International English Language Testing System is another examination system similar to TOEFL. Testing the same set of skills of a student whose native language isn't English. But unlike TOEFL it is evaluated out of a scale of 9
